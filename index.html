About Me

I'm a recent graduate with a Master's Degree in Business Informatics from Saarland University in Germany.

In my master thesis I worked on business process optimisation by developing a recommender system for operational decision support. The approach utlises real-time process analysis, taking both local process information and global contextual information into account.

Besides my studies I enjoy programming, photography and rock climbing. Also I'm a passionate Arch Linux user.
