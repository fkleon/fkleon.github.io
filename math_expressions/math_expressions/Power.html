Power class

The power operator.

Extends

Expression > BinaryOperator > Power

Constructors

new Power(x, exp) #

Creates a power operation on the given expressions.

For example, to create x^3:

pow = new Power('x', 3);

or:

pow = new Variable('x') ^ new Number(3);
